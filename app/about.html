About this website

Who made this website?

Hi there! My name is Angel Medrano and I am a web developer here in RI. When my daughter was born, my sleeping pattern changed. I found that I was up pretty early some mornings and decide to take up fishing. Being from Texas I wasn't really knowledgable about where I could fish in RI. I searched on line and found a few websites that mentioned places to fish but nothing really concrete. I called DEM and requested information and got sent a beautiful paper map from 2003 as well as a fishing abstract. Both where helpful but not really what I was looking for. So I decided to "roll my own" as they say. My hope is that this will provide anyone looking for a fishing spot quick and up to date information.

Where does the data come from?

All data on this website is publicly available on the RIGIS website. It was last updated in 2012. You can find the raw shape files here: http://www.edc.uri.edu/rigis/data/

Help me keep this up to date!

Did I miss a location? Is something wrong on the map? Help me keep this map up to date so we can all have a great time fishing! Feel free to submit any info you'd like by sending me an email asmedrano@gmail.com
